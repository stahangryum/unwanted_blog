Taylor & Maclaurin Series

Taylor Series

$ \begin{align*} f(x) & = \sum_{n=0}^\infty\cfrac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n \\ & = f(a) + \cfrac{f^{\prime}(a)}{1!}(x-a) + \cfrac{f^{\prime\prime}(a)}{2!}(x-a) + \cfrac{f^{\prime\prime\prime}(a)}{3!}(x-a) \end{align*} $

Maclaurin Series

$ \begin{align*} f(x) & = \sum_{n=0}^\infty\cfrac{f^{(n)}(0)}{n!}(x-0)^n \\ & = f(a) + \cfrac{f^{\prime}(0)}{1!}(x-0) + \cfrac{f^{\prime\prime}(0)}{2!}(x-0) + \cfrac{f^{\prime\prime\prime}(0)}{3!}(x-0) \end{align*} $

Examples

$ \begin{align*} &\cfrac{1}{1-x} = \sum_{n=0}^\infty{x^n} = 1+x+x^2+x^3+\cdots \\ &e^x = \sum_{n=0}^\infty\cfrac{x^n}{n!} = 1 + \cfrac{x}{1!} + \cfrac{x^2}{2!} + \cfrac{x^3}{3!}+\cdots \\ &sin\,x = \sum_{n=0}^\infty(-1)^n\cfrac{x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)!} = x - \cfrac{x^3}{3!} + \cfrac{x^5}{5!} - \cfrac{x^7}{7!}+\cdots\\ &cos\,x = \sum_{n=0}^\infty(-1)^n\cfrac{x^{2n}}{(2n)!} = 1 - \cfrac{x^2}{2!} + \cfrac{x^4}{4!} - \cfrac{x^6}{6!}+\cdots \end{align*} $
